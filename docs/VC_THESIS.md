# VC Thesis

The wedge is not AI content generation. The wedge is revenue action generation from proprietary customer feedback.

## Why Now

- Commerce teams have more channels than operators.
- Review and comment data is abundant but underused.
- Generative video makes creative production cheaper, so the bottleneck moves to experiment selection.
- SMB sellers want revenue outcomes, not another blank-canvas writing tool.

## Initial Customer

Chinese commerce sellers operating on Xiaohongshu, Douyin, TikTok Shop, Amazon, and independent stores.

## Narrow First Use Case

Turn product reviews and competitor complaints into a seven-day creative and conversion plan.

## Expansion Path

1. Review-to-campaign packs.
2. Creative testing memory by product category.
3. Channel-specific asset generation.
4. CRM and customer-support writeback.
5. Outcome-based pricing tied to qualified leads or GMV.

## Moat

The model is not the moat. The moat is the loop:

```text
customer feedback -> generated actions -> channel performance -> category playbook -> better actions
```
